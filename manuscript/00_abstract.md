# Abstract {#sec:abstract}

On-policy distillation is active inference at the level of a scoped variational correspondence: the posterior generates its own observations, and the generative model is conditioned on privileged beliefs. The intractable teacher policy plays the role of the generative model $p(o,s)$, the tractable student policy is the approximate posterior $q(s)$, and the per-token reverse-KL distillation loss is the variational free energy $F = D_{\mathrm{KL}}(q\,\|\,p) - \log p(o)$; on-policy student rollouts are the active sampling that minimises $F$, so the posterior is scored on observations it caused [@friston2010fep; @parr2022active; @levine2018rlinference]. This addresses the same induced-distribution failure studied in interactive imitation learning and sequence exposure-bias work [@ross2011dagger; @bengio2015scheduled], while treating privileged traces and feedback as training-time-only information in the LUPI/distillation sense [@vapnik2009lupi; @lopezpaz2016unifying]. We instantiate the correspondence in a minimal Active Inference stack on toy models: a Bernoulli–Ising analytical oracle whose coupling $\lambda$ ties a teacher's privileged variable to the answer (its mutual information $I(\lambda)$ is the teacher–student mutual information and its free energy is the distillation objective, with reverse KL recovering the mode-seeking self-distillation limit and forward KL the mode-covering SFT limit [@hinton2015distilling; @gu2024minillm; @agarwal2024gkd]), a pymdp T-maze rollout that is the on-policy student — an agent that generates its own observations and acts to minimise expected free energy under a privileged cue [@friston2019generalised; @millidge2020efe], and a sheaf-indexed compose contract that binds {{sheaf_track_count}} fragment tracks into {{composed_section_count}} flat IMRAD sections. The methodological contribution is a discipline rather than a domain finding: every reported number is hydrated from a generated artifact and every cross-track claim is machine-checked before rendering, so no figure or statistic can drift from the artifact that produced it — {{sheaf_law_count}} sheaf axioms are verified before composition and {{counterexample_count}} negative controls keep each failure path live. A two-agent classroom run pits a privileged teacher (cue validity {{classroom_teacher_cue_validity}}) against an on-policy student (cue validity {{classroom_student_cue_validity}}): teacher belief entropy {{classroom_teacher_belief_entropy_formatted}} nats sits below student {{classroom_student_belief_entropy_formatted}} nats in this toy system, with a mean reverse-KL distillation signal of {{classroom_mean_reverse_kl_formatted}} nats, matching the objective structure of recent privileged-context self-distillation methods [@zhao2026opsd; @liu2026sdpg; @penaloza2026pidistill]. Claims are limited to those models and their generated artifacts; the toy results demonstrate the correspondence rather than asserting anything about production LLMs, where external OPD evidence remains context rather than a reproduced result [@thinkingmachines2025opd].
